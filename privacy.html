Privacy Policy

New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. Privacy Policy is designed to provide clarity about the information we collect and how we use it to provide a better social networking experience. By accepting our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use in registration or application installation, you consent to our collection, storage, use and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Introduction

New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. develops and publishes social applications. In this policy, "New Hill Strategic Management Ltd." refers to New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. and its Affiliates, which shall mean subsidiaries, parent companies, joint ventures and other corporate entities under common ownership. We may also refer to New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. as "We" or "Us". This Privacy Policy describes: the information we collect, how we do so and the purposes of our collection how we use and with whom we share such information how you can access and update such information the choices you can make about how we collect, use and share your information how we protect the information we store about you If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our collection and use of information, please contact us as described in Section 14 ("Contact Us"). If you access our applications from a third party Social Networking Site ("SNS"), you may be required to also read and accept the SNS Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information about You Received from an SNS

When you use any New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. application at an SNS such as Facebook or MySpace, you allow New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. to access certain information from your profile for that site. The information you allow New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. to access varies by application and SNS platform, and it is affected by the privacy settings you establish at the SNS. You can control and find out more about these settings at the SNS where you use our applications. For example, New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. may access and store some or all of the following information, as allowed by you, the SNS and your preferences:

  • your first and last name
  • your profile picture or its URL
  • your user ID number, which is linked to publically available information such as name and profile photo
  • the user ID numbers for your friends that are also connected to our application(s)
  • the login e-mail you provided to that SNS when you registered
  • your physical location and that of your access devices
  • your gender
  • your birthday

By using an New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. application through an SNS, you are authorizing New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. to collect, store, and use in accordance with this Privacy Policy any and all information that you agreed the SNS could provide to New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. through the SNS Application Programming Interface ("API"). Your agreement takes place when you "accept" or "allow" (or similar terms) one of our applications on an SNS.

Information We Collect from You Directly

When you interact with New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. directly, either through a New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. application (whether or not on an SNS), at new-hill.co.uk, or any New Hill Strategic Management Ltd.-owned website, we may collect and store information from you directly as described below:

Registration Information

When you register with us directly, you will be required to provide at least the following information:

  • your age or birthday (for age screening and/or to better understand who our users are)
  • your first and last names
  • your e-mail address (which must be a valid e-mail address)
  • a password and other information that helps us confirm that it is you accessing your account

We may also offer you the option to complete a user profile that is visible to other New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. users. Your user profile may include:

  • a profile photo
  • one or more application username(s)
  • your gender
  • your interests and hobbies
  • your occupation
  • biographic details that you provide
  • links to your profiles on various SNS
  • details about the applications you use
  • a New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. user ID number, created by New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. and used to identify your profile

The New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. user ID number used to identify your account and profile will be public and will appear in the URL of your profile page, but will only permit access to information that is considered public or that you have designated as public in your settings.

Your first and last names and profile picture are considered "public" on New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. websites that offer direct access to New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. applications and search engines may index this publicly available information. When we offer profiles, we will also offer functionality that allows you to opt-out of public indexing of your public profile information.

We will also offer you the ability to import your address book contacts or manually enter email addresses so that you can (i) locate your contacts on New Hill Strategic Management Ltd.; and (ii) invite those contacts to join you in our applications. We will store those contacts for purposes of helping you and your contacts make connections on our site. If you do not want your contacts stored just follow the instructions available at the "Learn More" link. Passwords provided to New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. for the purpose of accessing your address book will not be stored.

Information We Receive or Collect When You Access New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. Applications on a Mobile Device

If you use New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. applications on your mobile telephone or other mobile device, we collect your unique device identifier and may collect your telephone number, country, and other information you choose to provide, such as user name, character name, location or e-mail address (i.e., to receive updates about the application).

Payment Information

If you purchase in-application virtual currency or goods from New Hill Strategic Management Ltd., we will collect the billing and financial information necessary to process your charges, including your postal and e-mail addresses, depending on your payment method. New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. may also receive the billing and payment information that you provide when you purchase New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. virtual goods or currency via a mobile device, such as an iPhone. We use and store this data in the same manner as billing and payment data collected from non-mobile visitors to our applications. Our Terms of Use explain our policies and terms relevant to our charges and billing practices. Please note that purchases of Facebook Credits are subject to Facebook policies.

Customer Support Correspondence

When you ask for assistance from our Customer Support team, we will collect and store the contact information you provide (generally your name and e-mail address), information about your application use or activity on the Service, and your user ID number. We will also store the correspondence and any information contained within.

Sweepstakes, Promotions and Loyalty Programs

We receive and store the information you provide, such as a user ID number or valid e-mail address or street address, when you enter any New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. application promotion, sweepstakes or loyalty program. Promotions which offer prizes (for example, other than virtual currency or virtual goods) will have separate requirements for eligibility and other rules, which you can easily locate and should review before deciding whether to participate.

SMS Notifications

We receive and store the information you provide, including your telephone number, when you sign up to have SMS notifications sent directly to your mobile phone. These messages notify you of in-application activity and may allow you to use the application from your phone. You can adjust the types of notifications you receive and when you receive them by editing your SMS notification settings; just log in to your application account and choose SMS mobile from the Help menu.

Technical and Usage Information

When you access our websites or use our applications, we collect (i) certain technical information about your computer system and (ii) usage statistics about your interactions with our websites and applications. This information is collected through the use of server log files and tracking technologies to collect and analyze certain types of technical information including cookies. Some of the cookies the Service places on your computer are linked to your user ID number(s). For more information on how we utilize cookies and other tracking technologies please review the "Cookies and Automated Information Collection" portion of Section 3 ("How We Collect Information about You").

Application Toolbars or Application Bars

Users may have the opportunity to download and install a New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. branded toolbar (the "Toolbar") when using various applications. This Toolbar allows you to monitor your real time status for New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. applications while you are browsing non-New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. websites. As the Toolbar is improved, it will be automatically updated on your browser. The Toolbar itself does not collect personal information. However, there are some features within the Toolbar that may allow you to provide personal information (e.g. Contact form, Tell-a-friend). Additionally, we may keep statistics regarding toolbar use on an aggregated basis. The Toolbar is based on technology provided by Conduit, whose privacy policy is available here.

3. How We Collect Information about You

We may collect information about you in any one or more of the following ways:

Information about Users and Usage

When you use one of our applications, New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. collects and stores certain profile information that you provide directly or through the SNS or other third party website that is offering our applications(s). To provide a richer application and social networking experience, we also collect information about your usage and interaction with other users and the Service. The bulk of this information is collected and stored through the use of web logs, which are files on our web servers that record actions taken on our applications and websites.

Communications Features

You may be able to take part in certain activities on our websites or applications that give you the opportunity to communicate or share information not just with New Hill Strategic Management Ltd., but also with other users of our Service. These include:

  • participating in user forums and message boards
  • posting public comments to other users' profiles or boards
  • sending private messages or invitations to other users, either directly on our websites or to their e-mail accounts
  • chat with other users
  • posting photos

We may record and store archives of these communications on New Hill Strategic Management Ltd.'s servers to protect the safety and well being of our users and New Hill Strategic Management Ltd.'s rights and property in connection with the service You acknowledge and consent to the recording and storage of such communications for these purposes.

Cookies and Automated Information Collection

When you access our websites or use our applications, we collect certain technical information in order to (i) analyze the usage of our sites and services which allows us to improve the service, (ii) provide a more personalized experience and (iii) manage advertising. We and service providers acting on our behalf, such as Google Analytics, use server log files and tracking technologies to collect and analyze certain types of technical information, including cookies, IP addresses, browser types, browser language, referring and exit pages, and URLs, platform type, the number of clicks, domain names, landing pages, pages viewed and the order of those pages, the amount of time spent on particular pages, application state and the date and time of activity on our websites or applications, and other similar information. In some cases, we will associate this information with your user ID number for our internal use. We may also employ other technologies including (i) web beacons, which allow us to know if a certain page was visited or whether an e-mail was opened; (ii) tracking pixels, which allow us to advertise more efficiently by excluding our current users from certain promotional messages or identifying the source of a new installation; (iii) local shared objects also known as flash cookies, which help us to prevent fraud, remember your in-application settings for features like music and to track your progress on certain in-application features. You can set your web browser to warn you about attempts to place cookies on your computer, or limit the type of cookies you allow. Flash cookies operate differently than browser cookies and cookie management tools available in a web browser will not remove flash cookies. To learn more about and manage flash cookies you can visit adobe.com and make changes at the privacy settings panel. If you disable cookies, you may lose some of the features and functionality of using our applications, as New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. cookies are necessary to track and enhance your application activities. Please note that companies delivering advertisements in our applications or on our websites may also use cookies or other technologies, and those practices are subject to their own policies.

Other Sources

We may collect or receive information from other sources including (i) other New Hill Strategic Management Ltd. users who choose to upload their email contacts, and (ii) third party information providers. This information will be used to supplement your profile - primarily to help you and your friends connect. It will be combined with other information we collect.
